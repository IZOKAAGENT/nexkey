## Lead Qualification

### Initial Response
```
Hi [NAME]! 👋 I'm the virtual assistant for [REALTY_COMPANY].

I see you're interested in [PROPERTY_TYPE]. Could you tell me:

1. 🏠 Are you looking to buy or rent?
2. 💰 What's your approximate budget?
3. 📍 Which area are you interested in?
4. 📅 When are you planning to move?

With that info, I can show you the best available options. 😊
```

```

I'm reaching out because we have new properties that match what you were looking for:
```
