## 🇬🇧 ENGLISH

### 1. Definitions

- **Personal Data:** Any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person
- **Processing:** Any operation performed on personal data (collection, storage, use, deletion)
- **Data Subject:** The individual whose personal data is processed (leads, prospects, clients)

### 2. Scope of Processing

NEXKEY processes the following categories of personal data:

| Data Category | Purpose | Legal Basis |
|--------------|---------|-------------|
| Name, contact info | Lead qualification | Consent / Legitimate interest |
| Communication history | Agent training & improvement | Legitimate interest |
| Property preferences | Personalized responses | Consent |
| Location data | Market targeting | Consent |

### 3. Data Processor Obligations

NEXKEY agrees to:

1. Process personal data only on documented instructions from the Client
2. Implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures
3. Notify the Client within 24 hours of any data breach
4. Assist the Client in responding to data subject requests (access, rectification, deletion)
5. Delete or return all personal data upon termination of services
6. Not transfer data outside agreed jurisdictions without prior consent

### 4. Security Measures

- **Encryption:** AES-256 encryption at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit
- **Access Control:** Role-based access, multi-factor authentication
- **Audit Logs:** All data access logged and retained for 12 months
- **Backups:** Encrypted backups retained for 30 days maximum
- **Testing:** Regular security assessments and penetration testing

### 5. Sub-processors

NEXKEY may use the following sub-processors:

| Sub-processor | Service | Location |
|--------------|---------|----------|
| Vercel | Hosting | US/EU |
| WhatsApp Business API | Messaging | Global |
| Twilio (optional) | SMS | US |

NEXKEY remains fully responsible for sub-processor compliance.

### 6. International Transfers

If data is transferred outside the Client's jurisdiction:

- Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) apply
- Transfer Impact Assessment (TIA) conducted annually
- Client notified of any new transfer destinations

### 7. Data Subject Rights

NEXKEY will assist the Client in fulfilling data subject rights:

- **Right to Access:** Respond within 30 days
- **Right to Rectification:** Correct data within 7 days
- **Right to Deletion:** Delete data within 14 days
- **Right to Portability:** Provide data in structured format within 30 days

### 8. Term & Termination

- This addendum remains in effect for the duration of the Service Agreement
- Upon termination, NEXKEY will delete all personal data within 30 days
- Client may audit NEXKEY's compliance with 15 days written notice
